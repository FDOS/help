Command: du

  DU displays disk usage, similar to UNIX 'du'

Syntax:

  du

Options:

  - none -

Comments:

  DU lists the folders that are within the actual folder, shows
  its size and the overall size.

Examples:

  You are in C:\FREEDOS:
    du              (lists all subfolders of C:\FREEDOS with its size),
    du | more       (lists all subfolders but stops after each page),
    du > C:\du.txt  (lists all subfolders but writes it to C:\du.txt).
