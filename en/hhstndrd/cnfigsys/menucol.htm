menucolor

Use before the menu config command, to create a full screen menu which supports the arrow cursor keys. 

   menucolor foreground[,background]
Where foreground and background can be one of the following numbers, to specify the menu color: 
     0      Black
     1      Blue
     2      Green
     3      Cyan
     4      Red
     5      Magenta
     6      Brown
     7      Light Gray
And the following colors can also be used on but for the foreground only: 
    8       Dark Gray
    9       Light Blue
   10       Light Green
   11       Light Cyan
   12       Light Red
   13       Light Magenta
   14       Yellow
   15       White
