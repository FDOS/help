unformat

Attempt to unformat a disk. 
   unformat [d:] [options]
Information from Mirror may aid unformat.

Options

  d:
  The drive to attempt recovery.

  /J
  Checks that data from Mirrpr
  is present and matches the disk information.

  /U
  Attempts to unformat a disk without Mirror.

  /L
  List every file and directory that might be recovered.

  /TEST
  Simulate a recovery, but do not attempt to recover the disk.

  /P
  Print the output on LPT1:

  /PARTN
  Attempt to recover the partition table.
