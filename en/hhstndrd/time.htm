time

Displays/Changes the system time. 
   time [newtime{A|P}]
   time /T
If no new time is given, the current time is displayed and you are prompted for a new time.

Options

  newtime
      hh:mm
      hh:mm:ss
      hh:mm:ss.ss
  The time to set for your system. 
  'hh' is the hour on a 12 or 24 hour clock.
  'mm' is the minute.
  'ss.ss' is seconds and hundredths of seconds.

  A | P
  Set the time to AM or PM if using a 12 hour clock.

  /T
  displays the time without prompting for a new setting.

Notes

When prompted for a new time, press enter to accept the current setting without changing it.
