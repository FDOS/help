CPI files (Code Page Information) Pack - DOS codepages


 This package contains Codepage Information Files with DOS codepages,
 which are of DISPLAY type.

 Below is the list of those files and the codepages they contain:



EGA.CPI

437   - DOS United States
850   - DOS Latin-1
858*  - DOS Latin-1 with Euro
852** - DOS Latin-2 (Eastern European)
853   - DOS Latin-3 (Southern European)
857** - DOS Latin-5 (Turkish)

*  The Euro version provides the Euro sign instead of the small dotless "i".
** This version follows IBM© standards, i.e. it provides the Euro sign
   in a codepoint which is left blank on their respective MS-DOS©
   versions.




EGA2.CPI

850  - DOS Latin-1
858  - DOS Latin-1 with Euro
775  - DOS Latin-7 (Baltic)
859  - DOS Latin-9 (= cp858 plus full french and estonian)
1116 - DOS Estonian
1117 - DOS Latvian




EGA3.CPI

771   - DOS Lithuanian and Russian (KBL)
772*  - DOS Lithuanian and Russian
855   - DOS Cyrillic-1
872** - DOS Cyrillic-1 with Euro
866   - DOS Cyrillic-2 (Russian)
808** - DOS Cyrillic-2 with Euro

*  Codepage 772 is identical to IBM© codepage 1119.
** The Euro versions provide the Euro sign instead of the international currency sign.

 Notes:

 Cp858 provides the Euro sign in place of the small dotless "i" found on
 cp850. All the rest between them is the same.

 Cp808, cp848, cp849 and cp872 provide the Euro currency sign instead of
 the international currency sign available respectively at cp866, cp1125,
 cp1131 and cp855. All the rest between them is the same.

 Please refer to the README.TXT file in what concerns the use of 5-digit
 codepages.
