bootfix

A bootsector testing and fixing tool. 
   boofix [/d] drive:
Where drive is the drive you wish to check and possibly fix.

Options

  /d
  Displays information on screen about the boot sector,
  including a hexadecimal readout.

Example

   bootfix /d c: >dump.txt
Will dump the information to a file called dump.txt.
