Tickle

Gives extra speed to floppy disk drives. 
    tickle
NB: LBAcache or a similar cache utility must be set up for floppy disk drives first. For instance, use LBAcache FLOP. Otherwise disk operation will be slowed down.

Notes

Tickle takes about 10k of memory.
